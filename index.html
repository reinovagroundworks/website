About Our Company

Reinova Groundworks is a sustainable construction solutions company founded in 2018 in San Fernando, Pampanga. We combine natural materials with pathway construction to create eco-friendly and durable products, serving clients across Central Luzon and continue to drive innovation in sustainable construction through research, development, and design excellence.

Our Vision

To lead the way in sustainable construction by redefining durability and design through nature-inspired innovation.

Our Mission

To provide eco-reinforced materials and customized solutions that promote environmental responsibility, structural resilience, and community development.
